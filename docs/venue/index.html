Venue

Potawatomi Hotel & Casino

MKE DOT NET 2017 will take place on the third floor of the Potawatomi Hotel & Casino.

The keynote, lunch, open spaces, and happy hour will take place in the Woodland Dreams Ballroom. Breakout sessions will be located in the Clarity, Harmony, Inspire, and Prosperity rooms.
